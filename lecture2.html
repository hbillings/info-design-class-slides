The Internet: a brief overview

A series of tubes

The Internet is just a network that moves data.

A series of tubes

  • Servers store information (mail, Web documents, images...).
  • Clients request information from servers.
  • Routers connect systems of various kinds.

A series of tubes

  • Data is sent between computers in packets, tiny fragments of information.
  • Packets can travel simultaneously and are reassembled when they reach the client.

Networking it

  • There are a variety of protocols for transmitting packets of data. The most commonly used is TCP/IP.
  • TCP controls the organization and transmission of the data between IP addresses.
  • Every computer, server or client, has an IP address.
  • DNS aliases IP addresses (127.1.1.0) to URLs.

Visual hierarchy

Hierarchy is...

The organization of items into different levels of relative importance.

Why is hierarchy significant?

  • Communicates information more effectively.
  • We respond more quickly to visual cues.
  • Creates order.

Figure/Ground

Figure: element in focus

Ground: element upon which the figure rests

How does this relate to infographics?

To tell an effective story, you need to lead the reader through all the pieces in the order that best tells your story.

The eye sees areas of shapes and colors first.

More specific visual details come next.

Individual words and phrases come last.

Alignment

Repetition

Establishes items at the same level in the hierarchy.

Baby names through time

In a group or by yourself

  1. Download baby name data for the past 100+ years.
  2. Take a look at the data, and pick ONE story to tell. (This can be by state, by year, by name...)
  3. Crunch your numbers. Isolate your variables. Get your data in order.
  4. Set some simple goals for your visualization based on the story you want to tell.
  5. Upload your data to Datawrapper and play with visualizations. Which types match your goals? Which work against them?
  6. Settle on a chart and customize.
  7. Publish and embed your graphic in the new discussion thread alongside your goals. (Complete for HW if you don't finish within the hour.)
