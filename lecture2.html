Gestalt principles

Proximity

Items near each other seem related.

Similarity

Items that look similar are perceived as a group or pattern.

Continuity

The mind extends patterns beyond the end points of individual components.

Closure

The brain fills in missing information about shapes and figures.

Figure/Ground

Figure: element in focus

Ground: element upon which the figure rests
